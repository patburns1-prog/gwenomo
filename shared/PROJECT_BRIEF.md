# GWENOMO — Project Brief

## Brand
GWENOMO by Wen

## Core idea
A playful, eye-catching, Spanish-first website for handmade decorative gnomes. It should feel magical, warm, premium-but-approachable, and memorable.

## Priorities
1. Outstanding landing page
2. Strong visual identity
3. Interactive feel without making it heavy or slow
4. Easy expansion into product catalog, custom orders, WhatsApp ordering, and seasonal collections

## Visual cues from provided assets
- Forest green logo typography
- Christmas / festive magic cues
- Handmade texture
- Soft cream / parchment backgrounds
- Warm red, gold sparkle, pine green accents

## Messaging themes
- Hechos con amor
- Ideal para regalar o decorar
- Guardianes del hogar / de la Navidad
- Hechos en Ecuador
- Disponibles en varios colores y estilos
- Pedidos por WhatsApp

## Suggested next deliverables
- richer product gallery
- about Wendy / the craft story
- collection filters
- custom order form
- seasonal product pages
- bilingual toggle if needed later
